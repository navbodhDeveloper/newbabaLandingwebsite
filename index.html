A Life Devoted to
Bharat Mata

Born in Rajgarh (Byavara), Madhya Pradesh, Baba Satyanarayan Mourya is a Gold Medalist in Fine Arts from Vikram University, Ujjain. Son of Smt. Bhagvatibai and Shri Lachhiram Mourya, his journey spans painting, caricature, graphic art, poetry, composition, singing, yoga, and social activism.

Founder of Bharat Bhakti Sansthan (Mumbai) and Baba Mourya Project, INC (New Jersey, USA), he has built a global network promoting Indian culture across 100+ international tours to the USA, Canada, Europe, Africa, and the West Indies.

He has performed over 1,500 cultural programs worldwide including Bharat Mata Ki Aarti, Rashtrakatha, and Ye Desh Hai Veer Jawano Ka — inspiring millions of Indians at home and in the diaspora.

Signature Programs

Cultural performances that have inspired millions across India and the world

भारत माता की आरती

Bharat Mata Ki Aarti

A grand devotional-patriotic aarti performed 1,500+ times worldwide — the signature offering of Baba Ji that unites devotion and nationalism.

राष्ट्रकथा

Rashtrakatha

A stirring narration of India's civilizational journey through poetry, art, and music that awakens national pride and cultural identity.

यह देश है वीर जवानों का

Ye Desh Hai Veer Jawano Ka

A heartfelt tribute to India's brave soldiers performed extensively for defense personnel and patriotic gatherings across the country.

दुर्गा से दुर्गावती

Durga Se Durgavati

Celebrating the lineage of Indian women warriors from Goddess Durga to Rani Durgavati — a powerful tribute to feminine strength.

शिवजी से शिवाजी

Shivji Se Shivaji

Tracing the spiritual and martial heritage from Lord Shiva to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj — a cultural and devotional masterpiece.

गंगावतरण और बाबा उवाच

Gangavataran & Baba Uvach

Mythological and devotional stage programs performed across India and internationally — deeply rooted in the Ganga tradition.

Key Milestones

A journey of devotion, art, and national service spanning over 25 years

1990s

Ram Janmabhoomi Movement

Active participation through art and poetry. Credited (reported) with the iconic slogan "Ram Lala hum aayenge, mandir wahin banayenge."

1990s

Zee TV — Religious Discourses

Joined Zee TV for six years, initiating the broadcast of religious discourses in Indian television media — reaching millions of households.

2000s

Bharat Bhakti Sansthan Founded

Established Bharat Bhakti Sansthan in Mumbai and Baba Mourya Project, INC in New Jersey, USA to promote Indian culture globally.

2000s

Nationwide Yatras

Undertook landmark journeys including Avira Ganga Yatra, Ram Van Gaman Marg Yatra, and Yuva Bharat Yatra across 16 states and 150+ cities.

Ongoing

1,500+ Performances Worldwide

Over 1,500 motivational patriotic cultural programs globally including Bharat Mata Ki Aarti, Rashtrakatha, and Ye Desh Hai Veer Jawano Ka.

2024

Ram Mandir — Ayodhya

Actively participated in the historic consecration ceremony of the Ram Mandir at Ayodhya — fulfillment of decades of cultural devotion.

यात्राएँ

Nationwide Yatras

Landmark cultural pilgrimages that connected communities across India

Avira Ganga Yatra

गंगासागर → गंगोत्री

Gangasagar to Gangotri — Ganga purification and water conservation awareness spanning the entire sacred river.

Apna Purvottar Yatra

ब्रह्मपुत्र → गोमती

Brahmaputra to Gomti — Cultural integration of Northeast India with the mainland, bridging hearts and histories.

Ram Van Gaman Marg Yatra

श्रीलंका → अयोध्या

Sri Lanka to Ayodhya — Walking in the footsteps of Lord Ram, a cross-border cultural and devotional pilgrimage.

Ram Purusharth Yatra

अयोध्या → जनकपुर (नेपाल)

Ayodhya to Janakpur, Nepal — Cross-border India-Nepal devotional journey celebrating the Ram-Sita bond.

Yuva Bharat Yatra

150+ शहर, 16 राज्य

150+ cities across 16 states — Youth awakening and tribal education initiative connecting the next generation to their roots.

Chandrashekhar Azad Yuva Chetna Yatra

झाबुआ क्षेत्र

Jhabua region — Dedicated to youth awakening and patriotic inspiration in tribal belts of Madhya Pradesh.
